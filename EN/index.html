瑞丽江的河水

Here is the personal website of 瑞丽江的河水 (my username in internet, which means water in the Shweli River). I come from Mangshi, Yunnan, China and reside in Ireland now. I am a Yunnan history and geography enthusiast, and also interested in gazetteers. I edited Wikipedia in my off school free time, and I was an administrator of Chinese Wikipedia from 2019 to 2021. I am currently studying the computer science honour Bachelor module at the Technological University of Dublin (TU Dublin). This website was built based on my Web Development 2 (PHP) course assignment, which was in the 1st term of the 2ed year. Please forgive I am not good at user interface design.

What can this website do?

  1. The "Library" has my e-books catalog, and I can cite them easily when editing Wikipedia.
  2. Anybody can ask for literature mutual aid on Yunnan topics, not limited to the e-books I already have.
  3. Practice web programming new skills I learnt.
